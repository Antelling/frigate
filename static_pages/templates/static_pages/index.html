Frigate

Frigate is an efficient and integrated way to organize notecards and write research based papers and articles.

Features

  1. Facts are organized into a notecard interface
  2. Sources are tracked, and are marked when they are used by facts and in the essay
  3. Cite notecards within the essay using parenthetical citations
